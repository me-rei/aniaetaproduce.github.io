We are partnered with...

Aetas from Zambales

The Indigenous group is known for its reforestation efforts and agroforestry practices, which are worth the recognition. The Aetas from Zambales use traditional and sustainable farming practices that produce good crops and preserve culture. This partnership is a powerful example of how Indigenous wisdom can lead the way in sustainable development and climate resilience.

Our Story

What Is Our Mission?

Bring the rich, diverse produce of the Aeta to larger markets.


From fresh fruits and vegetables to artisanal goods, every product at Ani Aeta reflects our commitment to uplifting Aeta farmers in Zambales. By embracing sustainable farming practices, we not only support their livelihoods but also nurture the environment. With plans to expand to more Aeta communities, we continue to grow together.

Celebrate the flavors of the Aeta cultural heritage.


We, along with the Aeta community from Zambales, take pride on offering our organic products. By choosing Ani Aeta, your support helps preserve the rich cultural heritage of the Aetas while promoting fair trade and environmental stewardship. Together, we create a positive impact—one purchase at a time.
